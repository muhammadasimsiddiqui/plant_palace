Product Returns Policy

1. Return Eligibility

Items must be returned within 14 days of receipt, unused, and in the original packaging. Proof of purchase is required.


2. Non-Returnable Items

Perishable goods, customized items, and sale products are non-returnable.


3. How to Initiate a Return

Contact us at returns@plantpalace.com with your order number to start a return process.


4. Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed within 7 business days to the original payment method.


5. Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. Contact us within 7 days to process an exchange.
