Wholesale Policy

1. Eligibility for Wholesale Pricing

You must be a registered business with a valid tax ID or resale certificate. A minimum order of 50 is required to qualify.


2. How to Apply

Complete the wholesale application on our website and submit the required business documentation. Approval is based on our review process.


3. Pricing and Discounts

Wholesale discounts are offered based on order volume. Prices may vary depending on market conditions.


4. Shipping for Wholesale Orders

Shipping costs for wholesale orders are calculated based on size, weight, and destination. International orders may incur additional customs fees.


5. Payment Methods

We accept payments via credit card, debit card, bank transfer, and PayPal. Full payment is required before processing the order unless otherwise agreed upon.
